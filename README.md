# Thiago Brommonschenkel

PhD researcher in **Plant Breeding and Quantitative Genetics**, focused on integrating **genomic and environmental data** to improve prediction of crop performance in **untested environments**.

My research combines **statistical genetics, environmental modeling and machine learning** to support modern breeding programs, particularly for **tropical maize**.

---

## Research Interests

- Genotype × Environment Interaction (G×E)
- Environmental Covariates (Envirotyping)
- Genomic Selection
- Spatial Modeling
- Machine Learning for Crop Prediction
- Cultivar Recommendation Systems
- Plant Diseases
